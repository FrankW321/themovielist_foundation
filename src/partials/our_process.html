How does itWork

Create an account

This is the first step to creating your The Movie List account. Fill your Email and password to complete your account sign-up process.

Search, filter movies

Use the search filter to specify movies you are looking for by changing: ratings, genres and sort by option.

Choose movies to get notified

Select the preferred option to recieve your notifications by Email or push-notification.

Set your personalized notification filter

For using this feature head to My Account settings to set the default notification filter to which movies you want to get notified.

Be the first who knows

Recieve your ordered notifications you have chosen by single movie or by your default notification filter set in My Account.

Enjoy the movies

Invite your friends and don't miss a single movie anymore!
